Elon Musk


Elon Reeve Musk (born June 28, 1971) is an enterprenuer and business magnate.
He is the founder, CEO, and Chief Engineer at SpaceX: early stage investor, CEO, and Product Architect of Tesla.
A centibillionaire, Musk is one of the richest people in the world.
